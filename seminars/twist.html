The Twisted Cubic

The Twisted Cubic (a.k.a. the other Algebraic Geometry seminar) is held on Mondays at 2:30pm in T420. Everybody is welcome.

We are currently looking at stacks. A good source of information is Andrew Kresch's book-in-progress available here.
