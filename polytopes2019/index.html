Lattice polytopes, with a view towards Geometry and Applications

ICMS, University of Edinburgh

18th – 20th September 2019

Lattice polytopes arise in many aspects of mathematics. This three-day workshop will focus on the combinatorics of lattice polytopes, with a view towards connections with algebraic geometry via toric and tropical geometry, and to applications.

Registration and funding

We ask that all people planning to attend register first. This will help us with logistics. Please register your intention to attend by e-mailing one of the organisers: Milena Hering or Alex Kasprzyk.

There is a registration fee of £30, with a reduced fee of £15 for students, payable at the workshop.
